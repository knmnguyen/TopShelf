Streetwear

JEFF STAPLE (Staple):

Independently created stuff. It wasn't streetwear yet. It was just people hustling, doing their own thing without any business or financial gain. Just expression.

JERRY LORENZO (Fear of God):

I guess anything would be considered street that comes outside of the traditional fashion system… and I still don’t really know what exactly ‘that system’ is.

EDISON CHEN (CLOT):

Streetwear is about culture. It’s not about clothing. It’s about stories. It's a lifestyle. It's how we feel. It's how we live. It's what we listen to. It's what we eat. It's how we spend our money.
Images and wordage taken from hypebeast.com and highsnobiety.com. For more information and to stay up to date with streetwear, follow the links to the left.

Topshelf is a story-telling website that focuses on the beginnings and the hype of pretigious street brands: Bape, Supreme, and Palace. Topshelf aims to provide readers with not only the story, but with visually aesthetic designs that would make way to a minimalist's heart.
